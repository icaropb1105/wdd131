Purpose

The purpose of this movie review website is to create a platform where movie enthusiasts can explore, review, and discuss a wide range of films. It aims to provide users with detailed information about movies, including synopses, ratings, and user-generated reviews. The site allows users to discover new films through popular recommendations

Scenarios

What are the top-rated movies to watch this weekend?

Can I see what other people thought about this movie before I watch it?
